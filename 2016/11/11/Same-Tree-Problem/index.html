Contents

Given two non-complete binary trees, the algorithm is to judge whether they are same (both in stuct and value or only in struct).

It’s my clumsy solution to build a queue for level traverse in that this only stores level information without parent information.

For most recursion, conditions should be simply considered (do not think too much!). For example, in this problem, we could focus on two nodes ($n_i$ and $n_j$) in tree A and tree B. These denotes two nodes are equal:

  1. $n_i = NULL$ and $n_j = NULL$
  2. leftChild of $n_i$ = leftChild of $n_j$ and rightChild of $n_i$ = rightChild of $n_j$ (and value of node)
